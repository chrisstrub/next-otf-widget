Step 1: Download Scriptable from the App Store
  1. Download Scriptable Install the free Scriptable app from the App Store.
  2. Copy the installer Tap the orange button above to copy the tiny installer script.
  3. Create a Scriptable script Open Scriptable, tap +, paste the installer, and tap Run.
  4. Run Next OTF once The installer creates a new script called Next OTF. Open it, run it, and log in with your Orangetheory credentials.
  5. Add the widget On your iPhone home screen, add a medium Scriptable widget and choose Next OTF as the script.
